Insurance Broker · Gauteng

AI Claims Agent — Pedro

An independent insurance broker was logging claims manually via email and missing after-hours incidents entirely. We built Pedro — a WhatsApp AI agent that captures motor and geyser claims 24/7, assigns reference numbers, logs every submission to a live Google Sheet, and notifies the broker instantly. The broker's team arrives to a structured log every morning. No missed claims. No manual capturing.

Technology Company · Gauteng

AI Outbound Email System — Thabo

A B2B technology company needed to reach hundreds of leads with personalised outreach — but their team was writing every email manually. We built Thabo — an AI system that takes a lead's name, company, and industry, generates a highly personalised cold email, and sends it automatically. Subject lines, body copy, and CTAs are tailored to each recipient. Every send is logged. The team now runs outbound at scale without a single email written by hand.

✓ Personalised at scale ✓ Zero manual writing ✓ Full send log
Lead Generation · Multiple Industries

AI Lead Fetcher & Qualifier — ICP Engine

A service business needed a continuous stream of qualified leads without manual scraping or cold calling. We built an end-to-end lead generation system that fetches leads from the internet based on an Ideal Customer Profile, scores and qualifies each one, logs all data to a central database, sends hyper-personalised outreach emails, and handles replies intelligently. The system runs autonomously — delivering a pipeline of qualified prospects every week with no manual input.

✓ Automated lead fetching ✓ ICP-based qualification ✓ Personalised email & reply handling
