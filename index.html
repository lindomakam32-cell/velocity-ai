What a live workflow can look like

A client reports an issue. The agent captures the right information, logs it, alerts the broker, and schedules the next step automatically.

Atlas — Claims Agent · Active
Client · 21:43
Hi, I need to report a burst pipe at my office that happened this afternoon. Who should I contact?
Atlas (AI Agent) · 21:43
Hi, I’m sorry that happened. I can help log this now. I just need a few details — what is your policy number, when did it happen, and was there any visible property damage?
Policy is POL-5319. It happened around 2pm today and there’s water damage to the ceiling and front wall.
Atlas (AI Agent) · 21:45
Got it. I’ve logged your claim under POL-5319 — Burst Pipe & Water Damage. Your broker will be notified first thing tomorrow. In the meantime, please take photos of the damage — I’ll send a quick upload link now.
